Donghoon Shin

I am an HCI researcher and a first year Ph.D. student in Human Centered Design & Engineering at the University of Washington. Currently, I am advised by Gary Hsieh as a part of Prosocial Computing Lab and DUB group

My research interest is at the intersection of HCI and AI / NLP. More specifically, I build systems that leverage AI / large-language models to lower communication burdens in various collaborative contexts, such as collaboration between ad hoc teemmates and science communication between researchers and designers

I did my undergrad in ECE and Information Science at Seoul National University, where I was supported by the Presidential Science Scholarship. I am the 2022 Korean Government Scholar for Overseas Study
